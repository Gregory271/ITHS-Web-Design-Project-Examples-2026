Shade's Skills And Move

Shade has five normal skill sets and one ultimate. All moves except defensive moves will gain ultimate but vary. Max Health: 10000
Skill Name Skill Short Description
Skill 1:"Shadow Strike" Offense: does 1000-1500 damage. This is heavy damage.
Skill 2:"Soul Siphon" Offense: does 500-750 damage and heals you. This is side damage.
Skill 3:"Veil of Darkness" Defense: defend all income attacks for one round.
Skill 4:"Crippling Cut" Debuff: Weaken your opponent by reducing their attack
Skill 5:"Night Step" Utility: Help gain your ultimate the fastest but does nothing to your opponent
Ultimate:"Abyssal Execution" Shade strongest attack move needing player to fill the ultimate bar to around 75 percent. It deals 3000 to your opponent.
