Alice's Skills And Move

Alice has five normal skill sets and one ultimate. All moves except defensive moves will gain ultimate but vary. Max Health: 10000
Skill Name Skill Short Description
Skill 1:"Ice Lance" Offense: does 900-1600 damage. This is heavy damage.
Skill 2:"Chill Touch" Offense: does 600-1200 damage and heals you. This is side damage.
Skill 3:"Frozen Ward" Defense: defend all income attacks for one round.
Skill 4:"Snowblind" Debuff: blinding your enemy by dropping their accuracy
Skill 5:"Blizzard Bloom" Utility: Help gain your ultimate the fastest but does nothing to your opponent
Ultimate:"Absolute Zero" Alice's strongest attack move needed the player to fill the ultimate bar to around 75 percent. It deals 3000 to your opponent.
