How To Play

You need two people to play this game. Each person chooses a character, Shade, Xiolin, and Alice Each character has their own special abilities like moves and passive. (Look specifically in each character skills and moves page) When the game starts, each person takes their own turn. They use basic moves which are offense, defense, utility, or debuff for each character they use. There is a limited amount of items each person has so use it wisely. There is an ultimate bar which is blue on the top and the player charges up by using an attack move or a utility move. Each player needs to deal damage to kill the other person. You can tell how much hp you and the opponent have by looking at the red bar. There is also status right under the ultimate meter. There is status like stun, weakened,
There will be a critic bar when you press on an offensive move. You will need to press the spacebar to stop. Make sure the line hits the middle so you will get the most critical damage.
Status: There will be different status depending on what you or your opponent did. There is Weakened, Blinded, Stunned, and defending.
Items currently have healing which heals which heals 200 health points.
