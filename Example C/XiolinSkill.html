Xiolin's Skills And Move

Xiolin has five normal skill sets and one ultimate. All moves except defensive moves will gain ultimate but vary. Max Health: 10000
Skill Name Skill Short Description
Skill 1:"Palm of Serenity" Offense: does 800-1400 damage.
Skill 2:"Chi Barrier" Defense: invurnable to damage from any incoming attacks for one round.
Skill 3:"Iron Body" Defense: defend any income attacks for one round.
Skill 4:"Pressure Point" Debuff: Stun your opponent giving a chance that your opponent cannot attack.
Skill 5:"Meditate" Utility: Help gain your ultimate the fastest but does nothing to your opponent
Ultimate:"Dragon's Wrath" Xiolin strongest attack move needing player to fill the ultimate bar to around 75 percent. It deals 3000 to your opponent.
