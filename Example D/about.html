About our Restaurant


Welcome to Kanikā Sushi, where tradition, creativity, and exceptional flavor come together to create an unforgettable dining experience.

Founded in 2009 by Katherine and Anika who have a passion for authentic Japanese cuisine and a love for innovative culinary artistry, Kanika Sushi blends time-honored techniques with fresh, modern inspiration. Every dish we serve—from our handcrafted sushi rolls to our delicate sashimi and signature entrées—is thoughtfully prepared using the highest-quality ingredients sourced from trusted local and international suppliers.

At Kanika Sushi, we believe food should be more than a meal—it should be an experience. Our chefs craft each plate with meticulous attention to detail, celebrating the natural flavors, textures, and colors of Japanese cuisine. Whether you’re joining us for a quiet dinner, a family celebration, or a special night out, we are committed to offering warm hospitality and a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home.
